About EthioWoodCraft

Based in Saint Paul, Minnesota, EthioWoodCraft creates meaningful woodcrafts that reflect the rich heritage of Ethiopia. We specialize in carving traditional Ethiopian imagery, symbols, and cultural elements into custom wood designs. Each piece is hand-designed with care, celebrating identity, roots, and creativity.
